\begin{abstract}
Expressions of the Calculus of Inductive Constructions carry a natural directed hypergraph structure in the sense of Barkeshli, Douglas, and Freedman.  We encode this structure in a runnable content-addressed CIC kernel and verify its equivalence with a traditional name-based CIC kernel.
\end{abstract}
