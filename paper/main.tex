\begin{abstract}
Expressions of the Calculus of Inductive Constructions carry a natural directed hypergraph structure in the sense of Barkeshli, Douglas, and Freedman.  We show that CIC type-checking is equivalent to growing a content-addressed directed hypergraph, and verify this against a traditional tree-based kernel on 15 declarations from \texttt{Nat} through \texttt{Nat.add\_comm}.
\end{abstract}
